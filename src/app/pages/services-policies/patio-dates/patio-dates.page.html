Patio Dates

Boarding and DayCare Pets Only

A Peace of My Heart (POMH) staff member will take your pet on a date to a local restaurant or coffee shop. These dates offer a unique special outing where they are pampered and enjoy the attention from other patrons and in some cases pets. Patio Dates must be requested on reservation form. There are some behavioral requirements for your pet to be permitted to go on these dates.

Two Forms required: Boarding Form or DayCare Form and Consent Form.
Patio Dates are subject to availability.
