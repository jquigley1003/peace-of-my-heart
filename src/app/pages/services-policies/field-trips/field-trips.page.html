Field Trips

Half Day and Full Day Rates

Peace of my Heart (POMH) offers two types of field trips: Half Day (up to 3 hrs) and Full Day (up to 6 hrs). These field trips are unique in that your pet will go on excursions away from POMH. Some include swimming opportunities or meeting different species of animals. Some of the destinations are: Atlanta Beltline, hiking at WD Thomson Park, privately owned property on Lake Hartwell, Desperate Dogs and The Pet Lodge.

Field Trips for Boarding or DayCare pets must be requested on reservation form. Dogs that are not here for Boarding or DayCare can attend, but reservations must be made via the Field Trip Reservation Form. Since the destinations vary, the behavioral requirements are different according to destination.

You may also request to be included in a group email which advises clients on scheduled upcoming field trips.

Two Forms required: Field Trip Form and Consent Form.
Field Trips are subject to availability.
