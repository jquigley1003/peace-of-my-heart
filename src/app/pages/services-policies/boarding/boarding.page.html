In-Home Boarding

Maximum Occupancy is 15 Pets

Your pet will stay in a home environment located in the Edgewood neighborhood of Atlanta. The pet will have around the clock care as it is also the residence of the owner of Peace of My Heart (POMH). This is a unique alternative to that of a large boarding kennel in that POMH only caters to a small number of pets at a time (max occupancy is 15 pets). Think of it as a bed & breakfast inn for dogs. Your pet is treated like a guest and will eat, play, and relax in a home with the owner and his pets. POMH offers a large privately fenced back yard where all pets get lots of play! There is a separate smaller yard if your pet is not comfortable in a group play setting with several other dogs. Pets are crated for their own safety when there is no one here (never longer than 2 hours), during meals, and at night time.

Before your pet leaves POMH, don't forget to pamper him or her with an exit bath! With this added service, your dog will enjoy a warm bath, clean ears, cut nails, and an external anal gland expression. Rates are based upon size and coat length (short hair or long hair). Examples of long coated breeds include : Irish Setter, Collie, Great Pyreness, Maltese, and any dog with similar length. Rates are listed below.

POMH has a very small staff, so we get to know your pet more personally. You can trust any special needs for your pet will be strictly adhered to.

Holiday In-Home Boarding

Holiday Boarding rates and a required length of stay apply during these holidays: Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's Day, Easter, Memorial Day, 4th of July, and Labor Day. Rates are listed below.

A minimum of three nights is required, to include one day/night prior to the Holiday and one day/night after the Holiday. This is a set rate, which includes up to five nights. So whether a 3, 4, or 5 night stay, the Holiday rate remains the same. If you wish to reserve more than five nights, the subsequent nights will be at the regular nightly rate. Discounts for a second or third pet from same household will apply.
